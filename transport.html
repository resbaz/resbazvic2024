How to get to Resbaz?

Resbaz will be at Monash University Clayton in the Learning and Teaching Building (LTB) on Level G (Monash Maps Link).
Monash University is readily accessible by public transport and cycle routes. Paid parking is also available on campus, although can be in high demand so driving is not recommended if you can avoid it. See here for recommended transport options.


Public transport

Monash University bus loop is just a minute or two walk to the venue, and has frequent services to the city and other suburbs.
Plan your journey with the PTV journey planner.



Cycling

Bike racks are situated just outside the Learning and Teaching Building.



Driving and parking

Car parking at Monash is generally in high demand, so we strongly encourage using public transport where possible.
Parking details are available here if you do have to drive. Casual parking on campus starts from $4 a day.
